Why Vansh's Pizza?

The gluten content of a dough is what makes it able to stretch out when being slapped out, and gives it a soft chewy bread like texture when baked.

Vansh’s uses a spiced sauce so you can tailor how spicy or hot the pizza is by asking for extra or light sauce
